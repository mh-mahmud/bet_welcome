# bet_welcome
Bet Welcome is a live betting project based on Bigbash, Caribbean Premier League, Super Smash, T20 Blast, The Hundred, SA20, etc. This project is for a foreign client who is located in Austria. 

🏟️ Project Overview: Bet Welcome
Bet Welcome is a premier live betting platform designed to provide real-time wagering across the world’s most elite cricket leagues. From the fast-paced action of the Big Bash to the high-energy atmosphere of The Hundred, this project delivers a seamless betting experience for fans across the globe.
🏏 Supported Leagues
Big Bash League (BBL) – Australia
Caribbean Premier League (CPL) – West Indies
Super Smash – New Zealand
T20 Blast & The Hundred – United Kingdom
SA20 – South Africa
And other major global T20 formats.
🌍 Strategic Considerations (Austria-Based Operations)
As a project developed for a client in Austria, the platform is built with a focus on several critical operational pillars:
⚖️ Licensing & Compliance
Adhering to Austrian gambling regulations and broader EU frameworks is central to our architecture. This ensures the platform remains compliant with local legal standards for online betting.
🕒 24/7 Time Zone Logistics
Because our coverage spans from New Zealand to the Caribbean, the platform is engineered for true 24/7 live monitoring. We ensure that data feeds remain active and accurate regardless of the time zone.
⚡ Real-Time Data Integration
To eliminate "betting lag," we prioritize high-speed API integration (via providers like Sportradar or Genius Sports). This ensures that live odds reflect the pitch-side reality with minimal latency.
💳 Localized Payment Gateways
The platform supports common European payment standards to serve its core user base, integrating secure methods such as SEPA, Skrill, and Neteller.
